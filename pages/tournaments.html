Required Format:
• Name (sequence1, sequence2, sequence3)
• Use R (Rock), P (Paper), S (Scissors)
• Order matters: 1st sequence = Round 1, 2nd = Round 2, etc.

Battle Resolution:
• Each round compares move-by-move until someone wins
• First win advances (not most wins)
• Example: RPRR vs RRPP
  Move 1: R vs R = tie
  Move 2: P vs R = P wins → RPRR player advances
• Identical sequences = tie (manual resolution required)
